Education & Research

I am a first-year master student at Department of Electronic Engineering and Information Science, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I received my BSc in computational mathematics from the School of the Gifted Young from USTC in 2023.

My research interest is in trustworthy AI, particularly deep learning theory and explainability, theory of generative models including diffusion models and LLMs, and their applications in optimizing algorithms. Here is my publication:
